## 🎉 YourFocus Has Reached 15,000 Users – Thank You!

Today marks a huge milestone for YourFocus on Steam: we’ve officially passed 15,000 users.
From the entire team — thank you. This project would be nothing without you.

When YourFocus began, it wasn’t meant to become a real product at all. It started as a tiny personal tool, something I created to teach myself Electron and experiment with the idea of improving focus during long gaming sessions. I never expected it to grow into one of my biggest projects — nor did I expect to build a community around it.

But here we are.

With over 1,000 beta testers helping us from the early days, YourFocus evolved far beyond its initial concept. Your feedback shaped new features, fixed problems, challenged old ideas, and taught us more than any tutorial ever could. Every suggestion, every bug report, every “hey, could you add this?” pushed the software forward.

A special shout-out goes to our hardcore users, some of whom have logged more than 900 hours of usage since October 2025. That level of dedication is something we never take for granted. You didn’t just use the tool — you helped define it.

And as YourFocus continues to grow, so do our ambitions. We’re already working on new features, quality-of-life updates, and deeper customization options inspired directly by your requests. This milestone is not the finish line — it’s a checkpoint.

Thank you for believing in an indie developer’s little experiment and turning it into something meaningful.
Here’s to the next 15,000.

Stay focused — and stay awesome.
The YourFocus Team
